Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a really easy to follow Wood Fired Pizza Recipe that you can easily make at home with just a few simple ingredients

The recipe is beginner friendly and takes no time at all, we can't wait to see what you think!

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Dough, can be premade or homemade
  • Cheese, mozarella is preferable

Steps

  1. Spread the dough out into a circular shape
  2. Get a ladel of tomato sauce and spread it an even coat on top
  3. Lightly sprinkle cheese over the sauce, adding a small amount of basil
  4. Cook for 10 minutes in a wood fired oven and enjoy!
